# Sum of Previous (SUMN)

Calculates the sum of the data values over a given period.

## Example

_SumN with period of 5._

| Tick    | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4  | 5  | 6  | 7  | 8  |
|---------|---|---|---|---|----|----|----|----|----|
| Price   | 2 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 6  | 4  | 3  | 2  | 5  |
| SumN(5) | x | x | x | x | 18 | 20 | 20 | 18 | 20 |
